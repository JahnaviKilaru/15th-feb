Xiaomi

Xiaomi Corporation (/ˈʃaʊmiː/;[2] Chinese: 小米), commonly known as Xiaomi and registered as Xiaomi Inc., is a Chinese designer and manufacturer of consumer electronics and related software, home appliances, and household items. Behind Samsung, it is the second largest manufacturer of smartphones in the world, most of which run the MIUI operating system. The company is ranked 338th and is the youngest company on the Fortune Global 500.

Realme

Realme company was founded on May 4, 2018.[1] Before that it was a sub-brand of Oppo, which is itself a subsidiary of BBK Electronics,[2] until its formation as a spinoff on May 4, 2018.[3][4] In May 2018, they released their first phone, Realme 1. On July 30, 2018, Sky Li announced his resignation from Oppo and his intention to establish Realme as an independent brand on Sina Weibo.[5]
